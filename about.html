About Me

I grew up in Old Tappan, New Jersey, a town about 15 minutes outside of New York City. I chose to come to Virginia Tech not only because I loved the college's atmosphere, but I also wanted a change of scenery. I've lived in New Jersey my whole life, and I've quickly realized how much I love the South. After graduation, I plan to stay down here to hopefully find a job in locations like Nashville, Raleigh, Charlotte, or Charleston. In my free time, I love to exercise, read, cheer on my Virginia Tech Hokies, and watch anything and everything on Netflix. I also spend a lot of time on social media!


This picture is from my last Hokie football game as an undergraduate. We beat our rival, UVA, the day after Thanksgiving and it was a night that I will always remember. Cheering on our football team in Lane Stadium will always be one of my favorite college memories!
