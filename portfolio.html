Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

In Creating User Documentation, I planned, researched, and wrote in diverse technical writing genres. For this project, I created a user-centered Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) that documents responsibilities as an executive board member in my sorority. Once the document was written, I conducted usability tests with potential users to assess its effectiveness.

Grant Proposal for Giles County Animal Rescue

In Grant Proposals and Reports, I created a project proposal for the Giles County Animal Rescue that asks for funds to implement a spay and neuter educational outreach program in the county's elementary schools. Through this project, I was able to work with a real life client and understand the proper format of grant proposal writing.

Wine Magazine Editorial Illustration

In Designing Documents for Print, I created an editorial illustration that complemented the content in a wine magazine article. This project allowed me to focus on principles such as color, space, proportion, repetition, and contrast. Also, I was able to get much more familiar with both the Adobe Photoshop and Adobe InDesign programs.
